FAQ — Houston

How do you handle humidity?
We use sealed, powder-coated frames and drainage-friendly mounting so humidity and cleaning do not affect the finish.
Are installs disruptive?
We prefabricate offsite and schedule tight install windows—often nights or off-peak hours for hospitality spaces.
Do you offer acoustic options?
Yes. Acoustic backing and dense foliage assemblies help soften sound in lobbies, corridors, and restaurants.
Can you integrate logos?
We design recesses and mounting points for flush signage, lighting, or digital elements without visible hardware.
