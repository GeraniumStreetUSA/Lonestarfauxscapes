## 4. Writing Style

### Voice & Tone
- **Professional** — Confident expertise without being stuffy
- **Trustworthy** — Clear, honest communication
- **Texas Pride** — Rooted in Texas, serving Texas
- **Premium** — Quality-focused, not discount-oriented

### Do's
- Use active voice
- Lead with benefits, not features
- Be specific (e.g., "NFPA 701 Method 2" not just "fire-rated")
- Include Texas city names for local SEO
- Use "we" and "your" to create connection

### Don'ts
- Avoid jargon without explanation
- Don't oversell or use hyperbole
- Avoid passive voice when possible
- Don't use "fake" — use "faux" or "artificial"

### Example Copy Patterns
- **Hero:** "[Benefit Statement]. [Proof Point]."
- **CTA:** "Start Your Project" | "Get a Free Estimate" | "Schedule Consultation"
- **Feature:** "[Feature Name] — [Benefit explanation]"
