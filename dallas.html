FAQ — Dallas

Do you coordinate with designers?
Yes. We produce shop drawings, coordinate logo placements, and provide samples for color approvals.
Are the hedges fire-rated?
We supply NFPA 701 Method 2 foliage with documentation for hospitality and mixed-use projects.
Can you light the walls?
We integrate LED-friendly framing and conceal wiring paths so lighting sits flush without visible hardware.
How long is install?
Typically 2–4 days after measurements; estate perimeters may extend slightly for large runs.
